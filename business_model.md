# AirTax Financial — Business Model

## Executive Summary

AirTax Financial is a premium financial services firm built exclusively for pilots, aircraft owners, and aviation professionals. We provide high-touch, specialized tax filing, tax planning, financial consulting, and insurance services—designed for the unique complexity of aviation income, deductions, and regulatory requirements.

---

## Company Overview

AirTax Financial exists to give aviation professionals the same level of precision, expertise, and personalized service they apply to their craft. Tax and finance for pilots are not generic—they require deep knowledge of per diems, crew rest, flight training deductions, aircraft ownership structures, and multi-state residency rules. We are the specialists who understand your flight deck.

### Brand Position

**Premium. Aviation-Focused. Trusted.**

We position ourselves as the definitive financial partner for the aviation community—combining institutional-grade expertise with the accessibility and personal attention of a boutique practice.

---

## Core Services

AirTax Financial began with individual tax filing and aviation-specific tax services. We are expanding into broader small-business financial support across the United States—offering startup and operational guidance to self-employed professionals, independent contractors, and small business owners who need a trusted partner from formation through filing.

### Tax & Individual Services

| Service | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Individual Tax Filing** | Full-service preparation and e-filing of U.S. federal returns, optimized for aviation income, deductions, and credits. |
| **Aviation Tax Services** | Specialized handling of pilot per diems, training costs, uniform deductions, multi-base scenarios, and aircraft-related tax matters. |
| **Tax Planning** | Proactive U.S. tax strategy sessions to minimize liability, optimize timing, and align tax decisions with career and life goals. |
| **Financial Consulting** | Retirement planning, wealth structuring, and financial advice tailored to pilot schedules, benefits, and career cycles. |
| **Insurance Services** | Life, disability, and professional liability coverage designed for aviation professionals and their families. |

### Startup & Small Business Support

| Service | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **LLC Formation Support** | Guided LLC setup for new business owners: structure choice, state filing preparation, and alignment with tax readiness. |
| **EIN Application Support** | Help obtaining an Employer Identification Number; review of need and alignment with LLC or sole proprietor status. |
| **New Business Tax Setup** | Federal tax foundation, deduction tracking guidance, estimated tax setup, and recordkeeping for new U.S. businesses. |
| **Bookkeeping Cleanup** | Organize income and expenses; categorization support, missing-record identification; preparation for accurate filing. |
| **Quarterly Tax Support** | Estimated tax guidance, quarterly payment planning, and ongoing check-ins for self-employed and small business owners. |

## Target Markets

### Primary Segments

1. **Commercial Pilots** — Major, regional, cargo, and charter operators seeking specialized tax and financial planning.
2. **Corporate & Private Pilots** — Part 91 operators, fractional owners, and high-net-worth individuals with aviation assets.
3. **Aircraft Owners** — Individuals and small entities with owned or co-owned aircraft.
4. **Aviation Professionals** — Flight instructors, mechanics, dispatchers, and industry executives.
5. **Startups & Small Business Owners** — Self-employed professionals, independent contractors, and new U.S. businesses seeking tax and formation support.

### Geographic Focus

- United States (federal and multi-state filing)
- Clients with aviation-related income or assets regardless of domicile
