# Brand Assets Needed Before Launch

Minimum brand assets required for website and marketing launch.

---

## Logo

| Asset | Purpose / Specs |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Primary logo** | Main brand mark; full-color; use on light backgrounds |
| **Logo — dark background** | Inverted or light version for dark headers/footers |
| **Logo — horizontal** | For header/nav; wide format |
| **Logo — stacked** | For square or vertical spaces |
| **Website header logo** | Header-sized version; clear at small dimensions |
| **Footer logo** | Typically smaller; ensure readability |

---

## Favicon

| Asset | Purpose / Specs |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Favicon** | Browser tab icon; 16×16, 32×32, or multi-size (e.g., ICO, PNG) |
| **Apple touch icon** | 180×180 for iOS home screen |
| **OG / Social preview image** | 1200×630 for social sharing (Open Graph) |

---

## Formats & Delivery

- [ ] Provide logos in SVG (scalable) and PNG (fallback)
- [ ] Favicon in required sizes (16×16, 32×32, 180×180)
- [ ] Social preview image in PNG or JPG; 1200×630
- [ ] Document acceptable minimum sizes and clear space
