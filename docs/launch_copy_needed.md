# Launch Copy Needed

Website copy required before launch. Use this as a checklist and brief for writers.

---

## 1. Homepage Hero

| Item | Purpose / Notes |
|------|-----------------|
| Headline | Primary message; 6–12 words; benefit or value-driven |
| Subheadline | Supporting line; 1–2 sentences; clarifies audience or offer |
| Primary CTA button text | e.g., "Schedule a Call" or "Get Started" |
| Secondary CTA text (optional) | e.g., "View Services" |

---

## 2. Services Intro

| Item | Purpose / Notes |
|------|-----------------|
| Services page hero/headline | Introduces services section |
| Services intro paragraph | 2–4 sentences; overview of offerings; sets tone |
| Individual service cards | 1–2 sentence description per service; link to full page |
| Services CTA | Encourages contact or scheduling |

---

## 3. About Section

| Item | Purpose / Notes |
|------|-----------------|
| Company story | Who we are; why we exist; aviation/small business focus |
| Mission or value statement | Short, memorable statement of purpose |
| Team bios (if applicable) | Name, role, brief background per team member |
| Why AirTax / Why Us | Differentiators; what makes us different |

---

## 4. CTA Text

| Location | CTA Text Needed |
|----------|-----------------|
| Hero | Primary and secondary buttons |
| After services overview | e.g., "Discuss Your Situation" or "Get Started" |
| After about section | e.g., "Work With Us" |
| Footer | e.g., "Start the Conversation" or "Contact Us" |
| Service pages | Page-specific CTA (e.g., "Request a Quote" or "Schedule a Call") |

---

## 5. Contact Section

| Item | Purpose / Notes |
|------|-----------------|
| Contact page headline | Welcoming; encourages action |
| Contact page intro | 1–2 sentences; what happens next |
| Form labels | Name, email, phone, service interest, message |
| Form placeholder text (optional) | e.g., "Select a service" dropdown |
| Form submit button text | e.g., "Send Message" or "Submit" |
| Success message | Confirmation after form submit |
| Email address | Visible contact email |
| Phone number (if applicable) | Visible contact phone |
| Business hours or response time | When to expect a reply |
| Scheduling link / Calendly text | If using online scheduling |

---

## 6. FAQ Questions

| Topic | Example Questions |
|-------|-------------------|
| Services | What services does AirTax Financial offer? |
| Pricing | How much does tax preparation cost? |
| Process | What is the tax filing process? |
| Documents | What documents do I need to provide? |
| Timing | How long does tax preparation take? |
| Aviator Network | Do you offer Aviator Network member discounts? |
| Geography | Do you serve clients outside my state? |
| Security | How do you protect my information? |
| Contact | How do I get started? |

---

## Notes

- Keep tone premium, professional, and U.S.-focused.
- Avoid placeholder text in final launch copy.
- Ensure CTA text is consistent where the action is the same.
