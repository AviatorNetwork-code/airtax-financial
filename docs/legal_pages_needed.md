# Legal & Trust Pages Required Before Launch

Minimum legal and trust pages AirTax Financial should have before launching the website.

---

## Required Pages

| Page | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Privacy Policy** | Explains what personal and financial data you collect, how you use it, who you share it with, and how users can access or delete it. Required for trust and often for compliance (e.g., state privacy laws). |
| **Terms of Service** | Defines the legal agreement between AirTax Financial and users: acceptable use, service scope, limitations of liability, dispute resolution. Protects the business and sets clear expectations. |
| **Disclaimer** | States that tax and financial services are not legal advice; clients should consult an attorney or CPA for complex matters. Clarifies scope of services and reduces professional liability risk. |
| **Refund & Cancellation Policy** | Explains under what conditions clients can request refunds or cancel engagements, and how fees are handled. Reduces disputes and sets clear expectations before payment. |

---

## Recommended

| Page | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Cookie Policy** | Describes use of cookies and similar technologies (e.g., analytics, forms). Needed if the site uses cookies; some jurisdictions require disclosure. |
| **Accessibility Statement** | Commits to making the site accessible and explains how users can request accommodation. Builds trust and may support compliance with accessibility standards. |
