# MVP Service List

Focused service catalog for first release. Keep launch-now lean; add services in later phases.

---

## Launch Now

| Service | Rationale |
|---------|-----------|
| **Individual Tax Filing** | Core offering; documented; pricing in place; Aviator Network integration ready. |
| **IRS Letter Assistance** | Natural extension; year-round; supports existing clients; limited scope. |

*MVP focus: One primary revenue service (Individual Tax Filing) plus one support service. All other services can be mentioned on the site as “coming soon” or omitted from navigation.*
