# AirTax Financial Project Scope

## Overview

AirTax Financial is a United States-based tax preparation and financial services brand.

---

## Current Scope

- Individual tax preparation in the United States
- Aviation professionals (pilots, flight instructors)
- Standard U.S. taxpayers
- Integration with Aviator Network for promotional purchase codes

---

## Out of Scope (for now)

- Canada tax services
- UAE tax services
- International tax filing
- Corporate tax advisory

---

## Brand Positioning

AirTax Financial is a boutique financial services firm focused on providing structured, confidential, and professional tax preparation with a specialization in aviation professionals.
