# Client Onboarding Process

## Overview

AirTax Financial’s onboarding process is designed to welcome aviation professionals into a premium, high-touch experience from first contact through engagement. Our goal is clarity, trust, and efficiency—so clients feel confident and prepared.

---

## Guiding Principles

- **Professional first impression** — Every touchpoint reflects our brand.
- **Clear expectations** — Clients know exactly what to expect and what we need.
- **Secure handling** — All personal and financial data is protected and handled with care.
- **No surprises** — Fees, timelines, and next steps are communicated upfront.

---

## Onboarding Stages

### Stage 1: First Contact

| Action | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Channels** | Website inquiry form, Aviator Network referral, phone, email |
| **Response time** | Within 1 business day |
| **Initial response** | Personalized acknowledgment; brief qualification (e.g., service interest, timing) |
| **Scheduling** | Offer discovery call within 5 business days |

---

### Stage 2: Discovery Call (15–20 minutes)

| Topic | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| Introduction | Brief firm overview and brand story |
| Client background | Career stage, income sources, aviation-specific items |
| Service needs | Tax filing, planning, consulting, insurance |
| Fee discussion | Provide range or estimate based on complexity |
| Next steps | Explain onboarding process; schedule formal intake if appropriate |

**Outcome:** Qualified lead proceeds to intake; others receive clear guidance or referral.

---

### Stage 3: Formal Intake

| Action | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Intake form** | Secure digital form covering: personal info, prior preparer, tax situation summary, aviation-specific items |
| ** engagement letter** | Scope of services, fees, terms, confidentiality |
| **Fee agreement** | Exact fee for engagement; deposit requirement if applicable |
| **Authorization** | Consent for e-file, information sharing (e.g., state agencies), and communication |
| **Deadline** | Signed documents returned within 5 business days |

---

### Stage 4: Welcome & Setup

| Action | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Welcome email** | Confirmation of engagement; introduction to assigned team member |
| **Secure portal access** | Login instructions for document upload and messaging |
| **Document checklist** | Customized list based on services and client situation |
| **Aviation checklist** | Per diem, training, uniforms, union dues, aircraft docs, etc. |
| **Timeline** | Expected dates for document due date, draft delivery, and filing |

---

### Stage 5: Document Collection

| Action | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Reminder sequence** | Gentle reminders at 7 and 14 days if checklist incomplete |
| **Support** | Offer call to answer questions or walk through checklist |
| **Verification** | Confirm all items received before starting work |
| **Status update** | Notify client when file is complete and preparation has begun |

---

### Stage 6: Engagement Kickoff

| Action | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Internal assignment** | Assign preparer/advisor; open case in practice management |
| **Client confirmation** | Brief message confirming work has started; reaffirm timeline |
| **Handoff** | Client transitions from onboarding to service delivery workflow |

---

## Required Forms & Documents

### At Engagement

- [ ] Signed engagement letter
- [ ] Fee agreement (or acknowledgment)
- [ ] E-file authorization (Form 8879) — collected before filing
- [ ] Identity verification (if required for portal)

### For Tax Preparation

- [ ] Prior-year return (if available)
- [ ] W-2(s), 1099(s), K-1(s)
- [ ] Per diem / travel pay summaries
- [ ] Training and education receipts
- [ ] Uniform / equipment receipts
- [ ] Union dues and professional fees
- [ ] Investment and retirement statements
- [ ] Real estate and mortgage statements (if applicable)
- [ ] Aircraft ownership / rental documents (if applicable)
- [ ] Any other income or deduction documentation

---

## Checklists

### Onboarding Completion Checklist (Internal)

- [ ] Discovery call completed
- [ ] Intake form received
- [ ] Engagement letter signed
- [ ] Fee collected (or deposit)
- [ ] Portal access granted
- [ ] Document checklist sent
- [ ] Client added to practice management system
- [ ] Welcome sequence sent

### Client Readiness Checklist (Client-Facing)

- [ ] I have submitted all requested documents
- [ ] I have access to my secure portal
- [ ] I know who my primary contact is
- [ ] I understand my fee and payment terms
- [ ] I know when to expect my draft / deliverables

---

## Aviator Network Members

For clients referred via Aviator Network or using a purchase code:

1. **Verify membership** — Confirm eligibility for member pricing or benefits.
2. **Apply discount** — Apply 10% tax preparation discount per pricing policy.
3. **Track source** — Record referral source for reporting and optimization.
4. **Same onboarding** — No shortcut on engagement letter or document collection.
