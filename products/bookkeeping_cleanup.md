# Bookkeeping Cleanup

## Overview

AirTax Financial helps clients organize messy financial records so tax filing and business reporting become easier.

## What This Service Includes

- Review of income and expense organization
- Categorization cleanup support
- Missing-record identification
- Preparation support before tax filing
- Recommendations for cleaner monthly tracking

## Ideal Clients

- Small business owners
- Self-employed professionals
- Contractors with mixed personal and business transactions
- Clients behind on recordkeeping
