# EIN Application Support

## Overview

AirTax Financial helps clients understand and complete the process of obtaining an Employer Identification Number (EIN) for a new business in the United States.

## What This Service Includes

- Review of whether an EIN is needed
- Guidance through the EIN application process
- Business information checklist before submission
- Help aligning EIN setup with LLC or sole proprietor status

## Ideal Clients

- New LLC owners
- Sole proprietors hiring workers
- Small business owners opening a business bank account
- Clients needing tax registration support
