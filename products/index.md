# AirTax Financial — Service Catalog

## Overview

Structured catalog of all AirTax Financial products and services.

---

## Individual Tax Services

| Product | Description | Ideal Client | Availability |
|---------|-------------|--------------|--------------|
| **Individual Tax Filing** | Professional preparation and e-filing of U.S. federal returns for W-2, 1099, and multi-income situations. | Standard U.S. taxpayers; pilots and flight instructors | Seasonal |
| **Tax Planning** | Proactive tax strategy to minimize liability and align decisions with business and career goals. | Pilots, business owners, high-income individuals | Year-round |
| **Self-Employed Tax Help** | Tax preparation and planning for independent contractors and self-employed U.S. taxpayers. | Independent contractors, gig workers, self-employed professionals | Seasonal |
| **IRS Letter Assistance** | Understand and respond to IRS notices—CP2000, verification requests, refund delays. | Taxpayers who received an IRS letter | Year-round |
| **Audit Support** | Guidance and documentation support for IRS examinations. | Taxpayers under audit or examination | Year-round |
| **ITIN Application Support** | Assistance obtaining an Individual Taxpayer Identification Number for U.S. tax filing. | Individuals without an SSN who must file U.S. taxes | Year-round |
