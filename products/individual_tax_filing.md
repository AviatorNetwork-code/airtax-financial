# Individual Tax Filing

## Description

AirTax Financial provides professional preparation and electronic filing of individual federal tax returns in the United States.

Services include:

- W-2 income filing
- 1099 income filing
- Student pilots and flight instructors income
- Multi-income tax returns
- Tax credits evaluation
- E-filing with the IRS
