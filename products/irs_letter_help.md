# IRS Letter Assistance

## Description

AirTax Financial helps taxpayers understand and respond to official letters received from the Internal Revenue Service.

Common situations include:

- CP2000 notices
- Missing information requests
- Verification of income
- Refund delays
- IRS identity verification

---

## Process

1. Client uploads IRS letter
2. Review of the issue
3. Preparation of response documentation
4. Guidance on submission to the IRS
