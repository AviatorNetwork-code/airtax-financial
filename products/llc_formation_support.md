# LLC Formation Support

## Overview

AirTax Financial provides LLC formation support for new business owners in the United States who want a simple, guided setup process.

This service is designed to help clients understand the steps required to form an LLC and complete the business startup process with more confidence and less confusion.

## What This Service Includes

- Guidance on choosing an LLC structure
- Help reviewing business name availability steps
- State filing preparation support
- Guidance on EIN application steps
- Basic business startup checklist
- Guidance on initial tax setup considerations

## Ideal Clients

- First-time business owners
- Independent contractors
- Self-employed professionals
- Pilots, flight instructors, and aviation-related service providers
- Small service businesses

## Notes

AirTax Financial does not provide legal representation.
This service is administrative and financial setup support only.
Clients may still need an attorney or CPA for advanced legal or tax matters.
