# New Business Tax Setup

## Overview

AirTax Financial helps new business owners set up the basic tax foundation needed to operate properly in the United States.

## What This Service Includes

- Federal tax setup guidance
- Basic deduction tracking guidance
- Estimated tax payment guidance
- Recordkeeping recommendations
- Startup checklist for tax readiness

## Ideal Clients

- New LLC owners
- Self-employed individuals
- Independent contractors
- Small service businesses

## Goal

Help business owners avoid common mistakes early and start with cleaner financial organization.
