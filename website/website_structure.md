## Page Hierarchy & Purpose

### Home

**Purpose:** Establish authority, communicate value, drive action.

**Sections:**
1. Hero вЂ” Bold headline (e.g., "Tax and Financial Expertise Built for Pilots"); subhead; primary CTA (Schedule a Call / Get Started).
2. Who We Serve вЂ” Aviation professionals: commercial, corporate, private, owners.
3. What We Do вЂ” High-level service overview with links to Services.
4. Why AirTax вЂ” Differentiators: aviation-native expertise, white-glove service, integrated offering.
5. Social proof вЂ” Testimonials, association logos, or partner badges (when available).
6. Final CTA вЂ” Schedule a discovery call; reinforce next step.

---

### Services

**Parent page:** Overview of all services; clear navigation to sub-pages.

**Sub-pages:**

| Page | Key Messages |
|------|--------------|
| **Individual Tax Filing** | Professional preparation and e-filing of U.S. federal returns; W-2, 1099, multi-income; aviation professionals. |
| **Tax Planning** | Proactive U.S. tax strategy; minimize liability; align planning with business and career goals. |
| **IRS Letter Assistance** | Understand and respond to IRS notices; CP2000, verification requests, refund delays; guided response. |
| **Audit Support** | Guidance and documentation support for IRS examinations; organized, professional representation. |
| **ITIN Application Support** | Assistance obtaining an Individual Taxpayer Identification Number for U.S. tax filing. |
| **Self-Employed Tax Help** | Tax preparation and planning for independent contractors and self-employed U.S. taxpayers. |
| **LLC Formation Support** | Guided LLC setup; structure choice, state filing, EIN steps; administrative and tax readiness support. |
| **EIN Application Support** | Help obtaining an Employer Identification Number; aligns with LLC or sole proprietor status. |
| **New Business Tax Setup** | Federal tax foundation, deduction tracking, estimated taxes, recordkeeping for new U.S. businesses. |
| **Bookkeeping Cleanup** | Organize income and expenses; categorization, missing records; preparation for accurate tax filing. |
| **Quarterly Tax Support** | Estimated tax guidance, quarterly planning, income review; keep self-employed clients on track. |

**Each service page:** Benefit-focused copy, process overview, CTA to schedule or get started.

## Content Guidelines

- **Headlines:** Benefit-driven, aviation-aware when relevant.
- **Body copy:** Concise; use subheads and bullets for scannability.
- **Imagery:** Aviation-appropriate (cockpits, flight decks, professionals)вЂ”or clean, abstract visuals that feel premium.
- **Testimonials:** Use real names, titles, and photos when possible; focus on outcomes and trust.
- **Trust signals:** Credentials, associations, security badges (e.g., secure document handling).

---

## Technical Considerations

- **Responsive** вЂ” Mobile-first; pilots are on the go.
- **Speed** вЂ” Fast load times; minimal heavy media above the fold.
- **Security** вЂ” HTTPS; clear messaging on data protection.
- **Forms** вЂ” Clear labels; validation; confirmation message.
- **Tracking** вЂ” Analytics and conversion tracking for optimization (per privacy policy).
